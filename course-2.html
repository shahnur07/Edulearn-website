About This Course

This comprehensive UI/UX design masterclass teaches you the principles and practices of creating beautiful, user-friendly digital products. Whether you're a beginner or looking to advance your design skills, this course covers everything you need to know.

What You'll Learn:

• Design thinking and user research fundamentals
• Wireframing and prototyping techniques
• Visual design principles and color theory
• Typography and layout design
• Figma essentials and advanced features
• Creating interactive prototypes
• Design systems and component libraries
• Preparing designs for handoff to developers
• Real-world case studies and portfolio projects

You'll work on real-world projects and build a professional portfolio that showcases your design abilities to potential employers.

Course Requirements

  • A computer with internet connection
  • Figma account (free version available)
  • Basic design knowledge helpful (not required)
  • Creativity and attention to detail
  • Willingness to practice and iterate

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Design Fundamentals (2.5 hours)

Learn design principles, user psychology, and the design thinking process for creating effective solutions.

Module 2: User Research & Analysis (2 hours)

Discover how to conduct user research, create personas, and understand user needs and pain points.

Module 3: Figma Essentials (3 hours)

Master Figma interface, tools, layouts, and all essential features for design work.

Module 4: Wireframing & Prototyping (2.5 hours)

Create low-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes to test your design ideas.

Module 5: Visual Design & Branding (3 hours)

Explore color theory, typography, and visual hierarchy to create stunning user interfaces.

Module 6: Design Systems & Portfolio (2 hours)

Build design systems and create a professional portfolio to showcase your work.
